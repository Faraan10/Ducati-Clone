The world's most attractive motorcycles are the result of a deep
commitment to racing competitions; they are the purest expression of
refined skill, unmistakable design and above all, a great passion for bikes.

Ducati is proud to represent Italian industry - the essence of Italian style
shows through the design of each bike. Sinuous, seductive and flexible
lines with a permanent, timeless quality that sets an unsurpassable
standard. Ducati Bikes – in over 60 nations throughout the world these
models dominate the dreams of the most ardent motorcycle enthusiasts.

The Desmodromic valve control system, the steel tubular Trellis frame,
the L-Twin engine and the unmistakable sound of the power unit – these
are the distinguishing features that underline the uniqueness of a Ducati.
